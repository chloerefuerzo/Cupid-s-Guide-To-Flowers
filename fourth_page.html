Calla Lily

The Calla Lily is associated with Hera, the Greek goddess of marriage, linking it to fidelity
and deep bonds. In Roman mythology, it was linked to Venus, the goddess of love, making it a timeless
symbol of romance. The smooth, flowing shape of the Calla Lily represents grace and beauty in love.
It symbolizes deep admiration and commitment, making it perfect for romantic gestures.


Lilac

Lilacs, especially light purple ones, are often associated with first love and the
excitement of new romance. Giving lilacs to someone special represents fond memories,
nostalgia, and a love that stays in your heart forever.

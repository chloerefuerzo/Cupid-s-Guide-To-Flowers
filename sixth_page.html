CUPID'S GUIDE TO FLOWERS

Flowers to give yourself


Lavender

Lavender is well known for its soothing scent, which helps to reduce anxiety,
stress, and tension. Keeping fresh lavender or lavender oil nearby can create a peaceful and
calming atmosphere, perfect for winding down after a long day.


White Lily

White lilies represent new starts, personal growth, and transformation.
If you're entering a new phase in life or seeking a fresh perspective, they are a great
reminder of hope and new opportunities. Keeping white lilies in your space can create a peaceful
and soothing atmosphere, perfect for self-reflection.


Daisy

Daisies are known for their bright, cheerful energy, making them a great way
to boost your mood. Their simple beauty reminds you to find happiness in the little things and embrace
a carefree spirit. If you're starting a new journey, career, or personal transformation, they serve
as a motivational reminder.
